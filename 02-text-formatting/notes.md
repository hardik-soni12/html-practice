## Text formatting  
  
▫️h1 to h6 for Headings. headings are used to show importants.  
    h1 ~~ most important.  
    h2  
    h3  
    h4  
    h5  
    h6 ~~ least important.  
  
▫️p for paragraphs.  
▫️strong or b : used for Bold text (semantic)  
▫️em or i : used for italic text (semantic)  
▫️u : used for underline text.
